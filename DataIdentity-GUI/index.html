What is PRAGMA Rice?

PRAGMA Rice is supported by funding from several institutions to implement the outputs from the Research Data Alliance by utilizing resources of the Pacific Rim Application and Grid Middleware Assembly (PRAGMA) community. Highly collaborative and community-oriented, PRAGMA Rice works with a rice genome group from the International Rice Research Institute in the Philippines, uses software services from the National Institute of Advanced Industrial Science and Technology (AIST) in Japan and connects cyberinfrastructure across the Pacific Rim region and the United States.


The goals of the project are:

  • Harmonize attributes and embed metadata in persistent identifiers (PID) for multiple data objects to harvest, preserve and cite data from varied scientific domains.
  • Provide a framework with both repository and PID service for long-term access and findability of data.
  • Propose a method to automatically harvest data from scientific workflows and improve reproducibility of workflow execution.
